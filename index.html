What is LifeCycle?

LifeCycle (a branch of young enterprise) is a  company set up by a group of young people, in Lisburn, Northern ireland. We aim to reduce the amount of waste we throw out by single use plastics on items that we would usually associate with being thrown away - such as instead of using plastic bottles, which harms the enviroment, to use our vaccum insulated stainless Steel company bottles that hold hot or cold drinks, and provide an enviomentally safe way to carry around liquids as well as cutting down the costs associated with buying plastic bottles of water just to be thrown away.  We also are selling Bamboo Toothbrushes, and bamboo straws, both of which are usually associated with being plastic, and being thrown away after a period of time.

We hope that through the sales of our products that we can encourage people to make the switch from plastic products to Eco - Friendly alternatives, that can be disposed of safely, and provide a sustainable future which will hugely reduce the amount of waste that goes into the ocean.
